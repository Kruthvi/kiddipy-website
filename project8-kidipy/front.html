Scratch Programming for kids Level - 1

Give a direction to your passion for animation and gaming. Learn coding from scratch. Make the sprites dance on your tune. Upload your program on the Scratch community and share your joy with programmers of your age.

3D Design and Modelling Cours

Tickle your aptitude for engineering. Join the beginner's level of 3D Design & Modelling course. Create your own 3D models of a spanner or a pen's body and more with a user-friendly 3D modeling program called TinkerCad. Explore endless possibilities of 3D printing.

Mobile Programming for Kids Level - 2

Create your own personalized music-player, steps-counter and shopping-list apps. Leverage accelerometer sensors of your mobile phones to calculate your steps in kilometers. Learn to build multiple in-app screens, add gif backgrounds. Program multiplayer games and language translator apps.

Microbit with MicroPython Programming for Kids Level - 1

Learn to program in MicroPython with easy syntax and fun way to control MicroBit. Do projects and solve challenges in an online simulator. This is a perfect course to switch to text coding from block-based coding. Share your projects with members of the international Python community.

Python Game Development Level - 1

Engage in fun game-based Python coding. Begin with Pygame to code your first interactive video game with multiple interesting backdrops. Learn typed codes of Python, HTML, JavaScript with graphical programming games. Code your sprites, define players and navigate them as you wish.

Arduino Electronics

The Arduino Electronics course is perfect for students who are looking for theme based practical learning. Students will be able to do all the projects and concepts taught in the session practically with the help of the kit.They will learn the basics of Circuit connections and gain awareness of the different types of input and output devices.

Python Programming with Animation Level - 1

Grasp better concepts of coding with fun. Draw graphics. Learn how to build your own interactive animation games with Python. Mentors of KiddyPi, explain concepts in a simple fashion. Participate in lots of practice tasks to learn hands-on.

Python with Machine Learning level - 1

Learn about AI/ML (Artificial Intelligence or Machine Learning) in a friendly guided environment. Train machine learning models to recognize images, sounds and classify numbers or texts. Create Python projects to achieve recognition and recommendation.
